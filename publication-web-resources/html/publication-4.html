THE IDL TEAM

Behind every redesigned learning space, workshop, consultation, digital activity, and academic

development engagement is a team committed to improving the student learning experience.

The ILD team brings together expertise in instructional design, learning design, digital

pedagogy, Moodle/Learn development, assessment support, accessibility, academic

development, and evidence-informed teaching practice.

We work collaboratively with academics, module coordinators, academic leaders, academic

development officers, and support staff across Colleges and Schools. Our approach is relational and contextual: each discipline, module, cohort, and teaching challenge requires a thoughtful

designresponse. The ILD team is therefore not only a technical support function. It is a teaching and learning partner that helps bring together pedagogy, technology, design, and evidence in sup-port of student success.

KHUTSO MNISI

My work centres on crafting meaningful learning experiences, supporting

academic staff, and shaping digital and blended learning environments that feel both intentional and responsive to context.

I am drawn to the evolving relationship between emerging technologies and education, particularly how AI can deepen and extend teaching and learning in higher education. My research, grounded in the Technology Acceptance Model, explores lecturers’ lived experiences with blended learning, paying close attention to the systems that enable or constrain adoption within the South African context.
