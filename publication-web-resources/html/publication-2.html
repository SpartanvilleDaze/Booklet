INSPIRING GREATNESS

The Instructional and Learning Design (ILD) team works with academics and teaching and learning stakeholders to design meaningful, accessible, student-centred, and digitally enhanced learning experiences.

The Instructional Design journey at UKZN has developed through phases of foundation-building,

programme development, refinement, and evidence-led practice.

Our work includes practical support with Moodle/Learn spaces, learning activities, assessment

design, accessibility, digital tools, and module enhancement. It also includes deeper conversations about pedagogy, curriculum coherence, student engagement, feedback, and evidence-informed

improvement.

The recent ILD journey reflects a growing shift: from visibility and support, to structured digital

curriculum transformation, to evidence-led practice and broader collaboration.

Original Instructional Design team assembled around mid-to-late 2021.
